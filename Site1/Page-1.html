WHAT IS OUR SUBJECT

Many young people are interested in fashion

I was worried and focused on people's experiences.
Everyone bought new clothes, but there's an error in how to wash them, or how to store them.
You may have ruined your clothes with the wrong knowledge.
So we wanted to create a service that would provide materials using fashion data, laundry services according to color, and storage methods. 

We divided category for fabric, color, lenth

If we take a photos our new clothes, then we can solve 
how to do wash service or preserve this clothes.
